About Us

Welcome you to our Institution- an institution with a difference. Jiban Jyoti Model School is on its journey of almost three decades in the world of child education. It is a journey to nurture the young minds following the model of traditional Guru- Shishya- Parampara. Our mission is not only to teach the day's lesson but to build character, to form good habits of the children that they would be ideal citizens of our country in the future and can return to the society in such a way that they would be regarded as the wealth of the nation. Ours has illuminated alumni as they serve in prestigious institutions as professors, engineers, doctors, teacher, and other important professionals. Modern approaches of "child-centric education" are adopted where facilities of dance, music, art, PT, and computer are given importance with their due weightage for the all-round- development of the learners. The school is situated in such an elegant environment that learners feel the atmosphere at its fullest form when the cool breeze of a large pond or green- paddy- field soothe their eyes, body and mind.One can find how the name of our school expresses its main motto as "Jiban Jyoti" means" the radiance of life". So we invite you cordially to visit our school where you can find the garden of little blooming buds and flourishing flowers in an Ashramite atmosphere.
